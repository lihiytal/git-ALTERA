Welcome to our website

what are we doing:

Our digital instrument is your gateway to the future of music. We've created a digital instrument with a touchless piano keyboard that fuses the ethereal beauty of a theremin with the limitless potential of a synthesizer.

The digital musical instrument in the image of a synthesizer, has 2 different reception areas that allow the user to play without touching: an infrared reception area (receiver and transmitter) which detects the position of the object in the reception area, and a distance sensor reception area in the form of a termin that controls the volume.

The function of the system is to produce music digitally in an innovative and convenient way.

The system will produce and play different types of harmonic sounds through a speaker, Adjusting/adjusting the sounds will be done by the website user.
